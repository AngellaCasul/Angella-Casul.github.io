Angella Casul's Grade Calculator

The website is designed to help students determine the average grade they need to achieve in their Midterms and Finals to pass their subject, given their Prelim grade.
Please enter your Prelim Grade and press "Submit" to see the results.

Grade Calculator

Grade Composition:

  • Prelim: 0.20
  • Midterm: 0.20
  • Final: 0.50
  • Passing Grade: 75
def calculate_grade(event): prelim = float(Element("prelim").element.value) passing_grade = 75 prelimpercent = 0.20 midtermpercent = 0.30 finalpercent = 0.50 # Calculate the required final grades current_total = prelim * prelimpercent required_total = passing_grade - current_total midfinalaverage = midtermpercent + finalpercent required_passing_grade = required_total / midfinalaverage result_text = f"To pass, you need a {required_passing_grade:.2f}% average in Midterms and Finals." Element("result").write(result_text) # Attach the event listener to the button Element("calculateBtn").element.add_event_listener("click", calculate_grade)
